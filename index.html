South American Palm Weevil Is Spreading in San Diego County

The South American palm weevil can severely damage Canary Island date palms and other high-value palms. By the time obvious crown collapse or major visible damage appears, recovery may be difficult or impossible.

Our service focuses on prevention, early monitoring, and homeowner education so you can protect valuable palms before the problem becomes expensive.

Signs Your Palm May Be at Risk

  • Drooping, thinning, or collapsing crown
  • Fronds suddenly dying near the top of the palm
  • Visible holes, damage, or decay near the crown
  • Nearby Canary Island palms showing decline or removal
  • High-value palms in areas where SAPW damage has been reported
