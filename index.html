Signs Your Palm May Be At Risk

Early treatment is critical. Once crown damage starts, recovery may be difficult or impossible.
  • Drooping, thinning, or collapsing crown
  • Fronds suddenly dying near the top of the palm
  • Visible holes, decay, or damage near the crown
  • Nearby Canary Island palms showing decline or removal
  • High-value palms in areas where SAPW damage has been reported
